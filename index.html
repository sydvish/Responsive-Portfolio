My name is Sydney Volk and I'm a full stack Web Developer. I have a Bachelors Degree in Organizational Leadership from Arizona State University and a Full Stack Web Development certificate from the University of Arizona.

I’m curious, and I enjoy work that challenges me to learn something new and stretch in different directions. The list of projects above will give you a good idea of the types of tools I’ve been introduced to and am comfortable using.

Professionally, my background has allowed me to do this by developing and maintaining training content for a fortune 500 company. In my personal time, I create as an artist and painter. Learning to code has introduced me to a new medium for creation and endless libraries of design and technology that has allowed me to evolve my natural abilities for problem solving and creativity into the digital world.
